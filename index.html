FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before you register. Can't find your answer here? Reach out to hello@devconf2026.dev.

What's included in my conference ticket?

Every ticket includes access to all keynote and breakout sessions, workshop seating based on your plan, conference meals and coffee breaks, a swag bag, and digital recordings after the event. Exact inclusions vary by plan — see the pricing table above for a full breakdown.

Where and when is DevConf 2026 taking place?

DevConf 2026 runs for three days this fall at our host venue. Full venue details, a downloadable map, and nearby hotel recommendations are available on the Venue page once your ticket is confirmed.

Can I get a refund or transfer my ticket?

Tickets are fully refundable up to 30 days before the event and transferable to a colleague at any time before the conference starts. Just email our team with your order number to make a change.

Will sessions be recorded if I can't attend live?

Yes. Standard and Pro tickets include recordings of every talk — Standard for 30 days after the event, Pro for lifetime access. Workshops are recorded where the speaker permits it.

Is there a discount for students or teams?

Students get 50% off Standard tickets with a valid .edu email. Bringing more than 10 people? Our Team plan covers groups of 10, and larger groups can contact us for custom pricing.

Do I need prior experience to attend the workshops?

Not at all. Every workshop lists a difficulty level in the schedule, from beginner-friendly to advanced, so you can pick sessions that match your experience.
